How to use?

Enter the amount you wish to convert in the provided text field. Then, select the currency you want to convert from (e.g., USDT, BTC, ETH) from the dropdown menu.

Choose the currency you want to convert your amount to from the second dropdown menu. You can select from various cryptocurrencies like BTC, USDT, or ETH.

Click on the Convert button to see the result of your conversion, which will instantly display the equivalent amount in your chosen currency based on real-time market rates.
